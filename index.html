Nourish + Bloom FAQ

How do I download the app?
You can download the Nourish + Bloom app from the App Store or Google Play Store. Simply search for "Nourish + Bloom" and follow the installation instructions.
How do I register my debit/credit card in the app?
Open the Nourish + Bloom app, go to the "Payment Methods" section, and follow the prompts to add your debit or credit card details.
How do I use the QR code to enter the store?
Once you have registered in the app and added your payment method, a QR code will be generated. Scan this QR code at the entry turnstile to enter the store.
What happens if I use the card-reader option?
If you use the card-reader option, there will be a pre-authorization charge that will be returned by your bank. You will only be charged for items you remove from the store, and no receipt will be printed. Your total will be reflected in the charge on your bank statement.
How can I get a receipt?
Receipts are available in the Nourish + Bloom app. You can view the breakdown of charges under the "Transactions" section.
